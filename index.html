Good To Know

Questions, answered.

What's the difference between Standard and Premium?

Standard is the honest everyday tiffin — 6 items in a reusable steel tiffin (₹100 refundable deposit). Premium is the full thali experience — 9 items including a sweet dish, gravy sabzi, dahi and papad — packed in a sealed disposable box with a spoon, so there's nothing to return and no deposit. Premium is one meal a day (lunch or dinner); Standard can be one meal (₹2350) or both (₹4700).

What is the ₹100 tiffin deposit?

Standard plan meals come in reusable steel tiffins — better for the food and the environment. A one-time ₹100 deposit covers the tiffin set, refunded in full to your UPI when you discontinue and return the tiffins. A small request: give your tiffin a quick rinse before handing it back — every tiffin is fully washed and sanitised in our kitchen anyway, but a rinsed exchange keeps things hygienic for everyone. 🙏

Can I skip a meal or pause when I go home?

Yes. Message SKIP before 9:00 PM the night before (for lunch) or 12 noon (for dinner). Going out of town? Send PAUSE with your dates. Skipped and paused days aren't refunded in cash — they extend your plan, so nothing is ever wasted.

Can I try before committing to a month?

Yes — that's exactly what the trials are for. Standard Trial: ₹270 for 3 thalis over 3 days. Premium Trial: ₹450 for 3 thalis of the full 9-item experience. Both come in sealed disposable packs (nothing to return), choose your lunch or dinner slot, no subscription, no follow-up pressure. Valid till 5 August 2026. There's also the ₹149 Express Meal for a single one-time taste.

Is the food pure vegetarian?

Yes — 100% vegetarian, cooked fresh daily in our FSSAI-registered home kitchen. The registration number is printed on every tiffin.

How do I pay, and is my advance safe?

UPI to the TableONE business account, with a WhatsApp receipt within minutes — plan, amount, start date, and our FSSAI number. If a month's advance feels like too much for a new kitchen, start with a trial. We'd rather earn your trust than ask for it.

When are Jain, vrat and snack items launching?

They're in development now. Message us on WhatsApp with what you'd like first — Jain thali, burgers, pizza, momos, whatever — and we'll notify you the day it launches. We build the menu around what people actually ask for.
