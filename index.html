Can I try before committing to a month?

Yes — that's exactly what the ₹200 three-day trial is for. Three lunches, no subscription, no follow-up pressure. The weekly ₹800 plan is the next step up. Both are available till 5 August 2026.

What is the ₹300 tiffin deposit?

Weekly and monthly meals are delivered in reusable steel tiffins — better for the food and the environment. A one-time ₹300 deposit covers the tiffin set, and it's refunded in full to your UPI when you discontinue and return the tiffins. Trials and Express Meals come in sealed disposable packaging, so no deposit applies.

A small request: please give your tiffin a quick rinse before handing it back. Every dabba is washed and sanitised in our kitchen anyway — but returning it rinsed keeps the exchange hygienic for our delivery partner and helps us keep every tiffin spotless for everyone. Thank you! 🙏

What if I'm out of town for a few days?

Tell us a day in advance on WhatsApp and we'll pause your tiffin. Paused days are adjusted in your next billing cycle.

Is the food pure vegetarian?

Yes. Our kitchen is 100% vegetarian, and it's an FSSAI-registered home kitchen — the registration number is printed on every tiffin.

How do I pay, and is my advance safe?

You pay by UPI to the TableONE business account and receive a receipt on WhatsApp with your plan details and our FSSAI number. If a month's advance feels like too much for a new kitchen, start with the trial or weekly plan — we'd rather earn your trust than ask for it.

Do you take bulk or office orders?

Yes — for offices, PGs and hostels with 5+ tiffins to one address, message us for group pricing.
